# FOR-EPIC Shopping Assistant

## Goal

Connect the nutrition plan to a realistic weekly shopping list and budget.

## Business value

A nutrition plan is only useful if it maps to real products, quantities and cost.

## Scope

- Mercadona product catalog
- Editable product URLs and prices
- Weekly shopping list
- Weekly/monthly budget
- Checked items
- Future price history

## Initial stories

- FOR-040 Mercadona product catalog
- FOR-041 Editable product prices
- FOR-042 Weekly shopping list
- FOR-043 Weekly and monthly budget
- FOR-044 Shopping checklist

## Acceptance criteria

- User can edit product price and URL.
- Shopping list estimates weekly cost.
- Dashboard can show food budget summary.
- No scraping dependency for MVP.

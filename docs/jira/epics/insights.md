# FOR-EPIC Insights Engine

## Goal

Turn body, training and nutrition data into simple weekly recommendations.

## Business value

Forma should reduce decision fatigue by explaining what to change and why.

## Scope

- Weekly check-in
- Trend analysis
- Rule-based recommendations
- Goal tracking
- Explainable suggestions

## Initial stories

- FOR-050 Weekly check-in summary
- FOR-051 Body trend recommendations
- FOR-052 Recovery warning signals
- FOR-053 Nutrition adjustment rules
- FOR-054 Goal tracking

## Acceptance criteria

- Recommendations are explainable.
- App avoids fake precision.
- Recommendations prefer small weekly adjustments.
- Dashboard shows one clear weekly insight.

# FOR-EPIC Training Engine

## Goal

Plan and track running and strength training.

## Business value

Forma should connect training load with body composition and recovery, instead of treating exercise as isolated activity logs.

## Scope

- Running progression from 4 km to 10 km
- Strength plan adapted to available home equipment
- Training calendar
- Completion logging
- Weekly training summary

## Initial stories

- FOR-020 Running progression plan
- FOR-021 Strength plan for home equipment
- FOR-022 Training calendar
- FOR-023 Mark training session completed
- FOR-024 Weekly training summary

## Acceptance criteria

- User can view the current running plan.
- User can view Push/Pull/Legs strength sessions.
- Exercises avoid gym-only machines.
- Completion is visible in the dashboard.

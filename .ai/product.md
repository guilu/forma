# FORMA Product Context

FORMA is a personal health and fitness application focused on practical progress.

## Product promise

Help the user understand what to do next across training, nutrition, body composition and shopping, using explainable recommendations rather than fake precision.

## MVP areas

- Foundation
- Body Composition
- Training Engine
- Nutrition Planner
- Shopping Assistant
- Insights Engine
- External Integrations
- UI & UX
- Platform & Infrastructure

## Primary user goals

- Track body composition beyond weight.
- Follow a realistic training plan.
- Eat according to training/rest days.
- Convert nutrition plans into shopping lists.
- Import measurements and activities from external providers.
- Receive simple weekly insights.

## Product tone

- Practical.
- Direct.
- Explainable.
- No medical diagnosis.
- No fake AI oracle behavior.

## MVP success

The MVP is successful when a user can:

1. Configure their profile.
2. Add or import measurements.
3. View training and nutrition plans.
4. Generate a shopping list.
5. Review weekly insights.
6. Trust the platform with personal data.
